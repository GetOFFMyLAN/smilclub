about smil.

SMIL. is a fashion and design company that specializes in creating the most unique merchandise designs on the market. The brand aims to provide individuals with comfortable clothing that expresses the individual's personality and creativity. SMIL. plans to release new merchandise every month that is either related to a specific theme or is completely different from other SMIL. designs. Some merchandise will only be available for a limited time.

The SMIL. brand was started by five 16 year olds: Gavin G, Will D, Will C, Nick C, and Axel R and is located in Texas. Each of the owners has their own role such as design, communications, and management.
